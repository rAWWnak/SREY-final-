ABOUT US

St. Thomas' College of Engineering & Technology proudly unveils SREY 2K24, Kolkata's most anticipated on campus tech extravaganza.
SREY 2K24 isn't just an event, it's an experience. Immerse yourself in a vibrant atmosphere brimming with electrifying competitions, insightful workshops, and thought-provoking talks. From coding challenges that test your mettle to design contests that spark your creativity, SREY 2K24 offers something for everyone.
But wait, there's more! Network with industry veterans, rub shoulders with fellow tech aficionados, and witness cutting-edge innovations come to life. SREY 2K24 is your gateway to ignite your passion for technology, spark innovation, and shape the future alongside the brightest minds.
Don't miss out! Join us at SREY 2K24 and get ready to be wired, inspired, and empowered.
